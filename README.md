# Kaiser AI - Your Personal Voice Assistant 🤖✨

Kaiser AI is a feature-packed voice assistant powered by Python, capable of handling tasks like web browsing, playing music, weather reporting, and much more. Designed for seamless interaction, Kaiser AI offers personalized voice commands, browser controls, and even some fun responses! 🎤🖥️

---

## ⚡ Features
- **Voice Commands**: Responds to your voice inputs and performs tasks such as opening websites, searching the web, or answering basic questions.
- **Play Music 🎶**: Plays random songs from your local playlist.
- **Weather Updates 🌦️**: Provides weather forecasts for any city.
- **Browser Automation 🌐**: Search on Google or YouTube directly with your voice.
- **System Commands 🖥️**: Control system functions like closing tabs, minimizing windows, or opening apps.
- **Fun Responses 🎉**: Get creative answers for fun questions.
